# fifa-cv possible states
Overview of all our different states and what characterizes them


Keep in mind that some states may differ thanks to substitutions!!!

## States
### Foul
Minimap disappears -> timer disappear -> ball is taken from static somewhere on the field

### Throwin
Ball goes over sideline -> minimap disappears -> ball is taken from sideline

### Shot over baseline
Ball goes over baseline -> minimap disappears -> keeper has ball

### Corner (attack)
Ball goes over baseline -> minimap disappears -> timer disappears -> square in the middle of minimap/flagpole/corner lines

### Corner (defence)
Ball goes over baseline -> minimap disappears -> timer disappears -> ball is taken from corner

### Offside
Minimap and timer disappear -> (ball is shot to attacker behind all defenders) -> ball is taken from static somewhere on the field 

### Kickoff
Ball is taken in the middle of the field -> everyone is on their own side -> no timer or minimap (red laliga in the minimap space)

### Halftime
Minimap and timer disappear at the same time (red laliga in the minimap space)

### Goal
Ball goes over baseline -> minimap disappears -> timer disappears -> kickoff

### Fulltime
Minimap disappears -> timer disappears
